something about

the mixologist

The Mixologist Restaurant was founded by the Culver family in 1984, which eventually branched out to more than 300 franchised restaurants all over the US. The Mixologist is well-known for its Filet Mignon, which made the restaurant extremely famous. They also have other items which include salads, sandwiches, desserts, etc.

we have

the best chefs

With 30 years between the three, our chefs Dave Johnson, Carie Bates, and Andrew Morgan have been passionate about cooking and delivering only the best meals to our customers. Dave, our head Chef is a no nonsense chef, every dish is inspected and given the Chefs OK by him.

If there’s one thing you need to know about our chefs, it’s this: They want to hear from you. If you have a question, a comment, a suggestion or a critique, let them know. They make a point of making themselves available in the dining halls, so feel free to walk right up and chat.

andrew morgan

Su Chef

Chef Andrew Morgan has cooked for some of the largest gatherings in St. Louis. At the Busch Stadium, Cardinals fans had a taste of his cuisine during the games, he catered food to baseball fans in the suites and Press Box, and even developed an appreciation for hot dogs. Now he creates memorable dining experiences at the Mixologist Restaurant for everyone to enjoy every day.

head Chef

With a culinary arts degree from The Culinary Institute of America and training in New York, Chicago and Detroit,Executive Chef Dave Johnson is truly A master of his craft. From Detroit’s elite kitchens he helped elevate the culinary experience at Michigan State Dining.

carie bates

head dessert Chef

Since her first job at age 15 in a German restaurant, Chef Carie has immersed herself in contemporary global dessert cuisines. She absorbs the culture and applies what she learns to recipe development. Chef Carie owes her culinary expertise to on-the-job training, chef mentors and formal training at Schoolcraft College. Her food philosophy is simple: use fresh, local, seasonal and sustainable products.
